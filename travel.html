Getting here

If you take the metro, Hauz Khas station is five minutes away from the school. Take the Exit 1 and take a left on Sri Aurobindo Marg.

If you take a DTC bus, it will drop you at The Mother’s International School bus-stop, which is directly opposite Gate No. 6.
